A restaurant is an establishment that prepares and serves food and drinks to customers, typically for on-site consumption, but also offering take-out and delivery services. They vary widely in style, offering a range of cuisines and service models, from fast food to high-end dining. Restaurants play a role in the hospitality industry, providing a place for people to enjoy meals and social gatherings.
